Process

Onboarded in two weeks. Shipping in three.

A repeatable, four-stage engagement. No three-month "discovery" phase. We move fast on purpose.

01

Apply & fit call.

30-minute call to understand the product, the goals, and the constraints. We either move forward or pass, same call.

02

Strategy sprint.

Two weeks. Positioning, content pillars, 90-day calendar, voice doc, asset library. Delivered as a working playbook, not a 60-slide deck.

03

Production & launch.

Content goes into production. First posts ship by week three. Daily story sequences from day one.

04

Optimize.

Weekly reviews. Monthly strategy resets. Quarterly redesigns of pillars if the data demands it. Compounding from day 90.
